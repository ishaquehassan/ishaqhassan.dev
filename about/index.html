About Ishaq Hassan

Senior full-stack engineer, Flutter Framework Contributor with 6 merged PRs into the Flutter framework, Engineering Manager at DigitalHire, creator of the Urdu Flutter course listed on official Flutter docs.

Ishaq Hassan is a senior full-stack software engineer based in Pakistan with 13+ years of professional experience. He is a Flutter Framework Contributor with six pull requests merged into the official Flutter repository and three more approved, making him one of a handful of South Asian engineers contributing at the framework level.

Professionally he is Engineering Manager at DigitalHire, where he leads mobile and platform engineering. Prior roles span Confiz, Tech Idara, Afiniti, and independent consulting. Over the years he has shipped 50+ production apps on iOS, Android and web.

Beyond code, Ishaq created a 35-video Urdu-language Flutter course that is listed on the official Flutter documentation. He has spoken at GDG Kolachi, Iqra University, DevnCode and multiple community events, and authored 7 Medium articles covering Dart isolates, Flutter's three-tree architecture, Firebase Cloud Functions, and native plugin development.
